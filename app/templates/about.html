About NowCloset

We're revolutionizing local fashion commerce by connecting style enthusiasts with neighborhood boutiques and independent retailers.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, NowCloset emerged from a simple observation: while online shopping dominated the fashion industry, local boutiques and independent retailers were struggling to reach customers in their own neighborhoods.

We believe that fashion should be personal, sustainable, and community-driven. That's why we created a platform that bridges the gap between local fashion entrepreneurs and style-conscious consumers.

Today, we're proud to support hundreds of local retailers while helping thousands of customers discover unique fashion pieces right in their backyard.
